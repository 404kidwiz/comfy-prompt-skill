# Style snippet: Editorial Vogue / High Fashion

Append to Look line for high-fashion editorial register.

```
Style: Vogue editorial fashion photography.
Clean composition with intentional negative space (top-third reserved for masthead).
Soft overhead key, gentle bottom fill, no harsh facial shadows.
Cool gray seamless or carefully chosen architectural backdrop.
Photoreal skin texture — visible pores, no airbrushed plasticity.
High-contrast tonal range, occasional single-accent color in monochrome palette.
Sharp focus on subject, shallow depth of field, slight medium-format softness.
Model poses: static, confident, eye contact direct or off-camera with intention.
Visual reference: Tim Walker, Steven Meisel, Annie Leibovitz Vogue era.
```

## Use cases
- Fashion campaigns
- Magazine covers and spreads
- Brand identity imagery
- Beauty / cosmetic photography
- Luxury commercial stills

## Compatible models
- Best: `flux-pro`, `flux-ultra`, `stability-ultra`
- Good: `nano-banana`, `stability-sd3`
- Avoid: `recraft` (too illustrative for fashion realism)
