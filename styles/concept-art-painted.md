# Style snippet: Concept Art Painted

Append to Look line for industry concept-art register (game / film pre-production).

```
Style: Industry concept art, painted digital matte aesthetic.
Visible brushwork, painterly edges, intentional looseness in non-focal areas.
Cinematic composition — strong foreground / midground / background separation.
Atmospheric perspective — distance lost in haze.
Dramatic lighting — single dominant source with deep cast shadows.
Color palette: limited, harmonious, narrative-driven (cool desaturated foreground,
warm distant glow for hope; or warm foreground cool distance for menace).
Hero element clearly defined, environmental scale anchored by figure or known-scale object.
Visual reference: Craig Mullins, Sparth, Feng Zhu — film/game concept art era.
Resolution: hero-frame quality, painted at concept-style proportions.
```

## Use cases
- Film pre-production keyframes
- Game environment art
- Fantasy / sci-fi establishing shots
- Book cover illustrations
- Mood boards for production

## Compatible models
- Best: `stability-sd3`, `stability-ultra`, `recraft`
- Good: `flux-pro` (with explicit "concept art painted" prompting)
- Avoid: pure photoreal models without painterly cue
