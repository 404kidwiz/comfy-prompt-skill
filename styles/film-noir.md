# Style snippet: Classic Film Noir

Append to Look line for 1940s-50s film noir register.

```
Style: Classic film noir, 1940s-50s aesthetic.
High-contrast black and white. Pure chiaroscuro lighting.
Single hard key light from above or side, deep impenetrable shadows.
Venetian-blind shadow patterns across faces and walls.
Cigarette smoke visible in light beams.
Rain-slick night streets, single streetlight pools of light.
Dutch angles for tension, low angles for character power.
Wide-brim fedoras, trench coats, period detailing.
Visual reference: The Maltese Falcon, Double Indemnity, Touch of Evil.
Composition: hard verticals and diagonals, asymmetric framing.
Heavy 35mm film grain.
```

## Use cases
- Detective / mystery
- Crime drama
- Period thrillers
- Femme fatale portraits
- Stylized monochrome editorial

## Compatible models
- Best: `flux-pro`, `flux-ultra` (chiaroscuro fidelity)
- Good: `stability-sd3`, `nano-banana`
- Video: `seedance` (handles monochrome + grain well)
