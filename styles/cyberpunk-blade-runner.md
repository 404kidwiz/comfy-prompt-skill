# Style snippet: Cyberpunk / Blade Runner

Append to Look line for cyberpunk / neon-noir register.

```
Style: Photoreal cyberpunk neon-noir. Blade Runner 2049 influence.
Dominant neon practical lighting — cyan, magenta, amber holograms cycling.
Atmospheric haze and rain. Wet reflective surfaces — pavement, glass, metal.
Lens flares from neon signs, slight chromatic aberration.
Anamorphic widescreen feel, letterboxed black bars implied.
Color palette: deep teal-cyan dominant, magenta and amber accents, crushed blacks.
Layered depth — foreground rain, mid-ground figure, distant city silhouette.
Hologram light sources cast colored rim light on subject.
Heavy atmospheric perspective, distant elements lost in haze.
```

## Use cases
- Futuristic city environments
- Lone-figure-in-neon-alley shots
- Tech / hacker imagery
- Dystopian commercial environments
- Synthwave / vaporwave overlap

## Compatible models
- Best: `flux-pro`, `flux-ultra`, `nano-banana`
- Good: `stability-sd3` (slightly painterly version)
- Video: `seedance`, `grok-video`
